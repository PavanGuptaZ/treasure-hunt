Hider Turn

Hide objects by dragging and start the Game

Message box

In the game, two players take turns playing as either the hider or the seeker. The hider hides a gold object and a bomb in one of 12 empty boxes. The seeker has three tries to find the box with the gold. If they choose the box with the bomb, they lose the game. The seeker needs to be careful and use their luck and thinking skills to pick the right box and win the game.
